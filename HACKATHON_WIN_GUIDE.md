## Form fields

**Team name:** Team Digital Voices Uganda

**Project name:** Sauti — Uganda Youth Internet Governance Platform

**Describe your project briefly (max 200 words):**

Sauti (Swahili: "voice") closes the internet governance awareness gap by combining applied digital-harm literacy with live youth consultation. Unlike standard awareness tools, Sauti trains young Ugandans to spot misinformation, deepfakes, financial scams, data exploitation, and privacy violations through realistic Ugandan scenarios using the TRACE method (Trust, Red flags, Ask, Consent, Evidence). Its Voice module captures youth positions on AI content labelling, app data transparency, digital literacy investment, privacy enforcement, and UYIGF youth representation. Responses aggregate in real time into a downloadable Youth Digital Governance Brief — a concrete artifact for UIGF/UYIGF thematic input and policy dialogue. Governance Pulse connects youth to active UIGF and UCC processes. Sauti moves youth from passive digital consumers to contributors in national internet governance, supporting UYIGF 2026 goals of AI-policy capacity, amplified youth voice, and tangible innovation. Mobile-first, built for schools and communities beyond the hackathon.

## 30-second pitch (pre-screening)

> "Sauti means voice. We didn't build another awareness app — we built a **youth consultation instrument**. Youth learn internet governance, practice spotting digital harms with our TRACE method on real Ugandan scenarios, answer pulse polls on AI and data policy, and their answers compile into a **Youth Digital Governance Brief** you can download right now — live data, submittable to UIGF. Watch: I answer one poll… the brief updates… and we export the PDF. **Your voice. Uganda's digital future.**"

## 3-minute demo script

1. **Landing (20s)** — Show live counter, Governance Pulse, UYIGF alignment. Say: *"We track governance processes AND capture youth input."*
2. **Spot It (60s)** — Open TRACE intro, do 1 scenario fast. Name the harm type. Say: *"Applied AI literacy — not trivia."*
3. **Voice (30s)** — Have a teammate answer a poll on their phone.
4. **Brief (60s)** — Refresh, show chart move, download PDF. Say: *"This is evidence for UIGF thematic input."*
5. **Close (10s)** — Team Digital Voices Uganda. Grand prize ask.

- Don't apologize for UI — lead with the live brief demo
- Don't fake audience numbers — use real multi-phone demo
